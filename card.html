Lion

A lion is a very powerful built cat that is second in size only to the tiger. The lion has been one of the best known wild animal since earliest times.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Leo
  • Average Length: 2.6 to 3.3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Grassland, savana, & woodland

The lion is a well-muscled cat with a long body, large head, and short legs. Size and appearance vary considerably between the sexes. The lion's coat is short and varies in color from buffy yellow, orange-brown, or silver grey to dark brown with a tuft on the tail tip that is usually darker than the rest of the coat.
